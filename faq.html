Q. I have some friends visiting. Do you offer guest passes?

  We offer day passes to anyone who is not a member for $10. Individuals are only allowed to use the day pass up until midnight the next day.



Q. Do you offer a trial membership if I’m not sure that I want to join?

  Our trial offering is a great opportunity for new members to enjoy Skillshare Premium prior to becoming a paid member. Once your trial period ends, you will be charged either a monthly or  annual fee, depending on the membership plan you selected when signing up.

  Don't worry we are here to help you out.
